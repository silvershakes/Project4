LET’S UNDERSTAND EXCHANGE AT

MARUTI SUZUKI


Selling an old car is a long and cumbersome process. The process further becomes stressful when you plan to buy a new car in exchange of your old car. Here comes the exchange process at Maruti Suzuki handy. We buy your old car in exchange for a new car. The old car can be of any manufacturer and manufactured in any year. To make the transaction easy, the value of the old car can be used towards the payment of new Maruti Suzuki car. The large network of Maruti Suzuki makes it easy for you to evaluate your old car. You can get it evaluated at any dealership of Maruti Suzuki. The exchange process does not end here. We also ensure that the old car is sold properly with complete documentation. Now that’s exchange for you: convenient, transparent and reliable.

LET’S UNDERSTAND EXCHANGE AT

  • Bring your existing car at any Maruti Suzuki dealership for Evaluation

    Evaluation is carried out by expert using digital App on 376 check points

    Based on the digital evaluation report the price of your car is determined and quoted

    Once the price is agreed upon, the car needs to be handed over to the dealership along with 2 keys and documents*

    A certificate of undertaking (purchase agreement) along with receipt of old car payment is issued.

    The Price as agreed upon for the old car can be adjusted against the price of new car

    Existing customer is intimated about the status of sale of old car on regular basis.

  • 1. Address proof

    4. Original invoice and Valid Insurance

    7. Duly Signed Form 29 & 30

    8. Bank Hypothecation closure statement and Form 35
